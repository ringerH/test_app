def getMaxVol(money, price, volume, n):
K = []
for i in range(n + 1):
temp = []
for j in range(money + 1):
temp.append(0)
K.append(temp)
for i in range(n + 1):
for m in range(money + 1):
if i == 0 or m == 0:
K[i][m] = 0
elif price[i - 1] <= m:
K[i][m] = max(volume[i - 1] + K[i - 1][m - price[i - 1]], K[i - 1][m])
else:
K[i][m] = K[i - 1][m]
return K[n][money]

Question 10 – A statement is given followed by two arguments. Decide which of the
arguments is/are strong with respect to the statement?
Statements:
In schools, primary level education should be given in the mother tongue of the
students
Arguments
1. Students can learn very easily in their mother language.
2. Students will feel more comfortable while thinking and absorbing the knowledge
provided by the teacher.
Options
1. Both arguments I and II are strong
2. Argument I is strong
3. Neither argument I nor II is strong
4. Argument II is strong
Answer: a – Both arguments I and II are strong.
